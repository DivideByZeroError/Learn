TAGS

. A tag is a term that
starts with < and ends with a >

example: <body>

. Most tags need to have a closing tag.
A closing tag is used at when you are done putting information into a tag

example: <body> this is the text for the body of my page

</body>

this here is what a closing tag should look like. you take the tag you want to close and at the end of the information you want to put in it you put the tag in then you just add a slash.
